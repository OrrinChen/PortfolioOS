# Project Freeze: Paper-Stage Only

As of 2026-04-08, the project is entering a frozen operating mode outside the paper stage.

This is a project-level coordination decision, not a judgment that the research work was useless. The research lines have already produced their main method assets, closeout notes, archived negatives, and promotion-contract boundary. The execution platform is stable enough to support paper-stage work without continuing to expand research scope.

## Operating Rule

The only active project lane is now the paper stage.

Everything else is frozen by default:

- no new research hypotheses,
- no new Stage 1 / Stage 2 / Stage 3 branch generation,
- no new A4 / A5 tuning,
- no new Qlib-to-PortfolioOS integration work,
- no repo merge work.

## What Remains Live

The following remain live only insofar as they support the paper stage:

- paper execution path,
- paper calibration and monitoring,
- stable platform maintenance directly needed by paper-stage operation,
- reading archived research outputs when paper-stage interpretation needs context.

## What This Means For Research

US and A-share research are both now treated as frozen archives unless an explicit future decision reopens one of them.

The promotion-contract work completed on 2026-04-08 remains valid, but it is now a boundary artifact rather than an invitation to continue integration.

## Reopen Rule

No frozen area reopens by inertia.

Reopen requires an explicit decision with a named objective, rather than a nearby variant, deferred branch, or unfinished idea.
